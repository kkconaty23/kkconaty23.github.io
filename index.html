MAKE ME BALD!

Hello friends! My name is Kevin Conaty and for those of you who do not know my younger brother was diagnosed with brain cancer when he was 2 years old. He has now been cancer free for 14 years! Many amazing charities exist out there, but one that my family has been involved with for many years is St. Baldricks, a not-for-profit organization, with the aim of raising funds to help find cures for children with cancer. This year the event is held on March 8th at Berner Middle School, and I want to raise money for this cause. How much you ask? That depends on how bad you all want to see me shave this mane! I have been growing my hair out for 3 years and it finally has a purpose. I will be sure to reach out to anyone who does make a donation to remind you of the details of the event so you can enjoy the shave show, any donor who is not able to attend will receive a photo receipt of my bald head. No donation is too big or too small, and 100% of contributions will go towards finding cures for children with cancer through the St. Baldricks foundation. Thank you all ❤
